Data Dictionary – Transformed UCI Data set

Variable labels are composed of the following components

Prefix
	Time		Raw time domain signals
	Fourier		The result of a Fast Fourier Transform (FFT) of the raw time domain signals
	
Source
	Gravity		Baseline gravity effects
	Body		Signals generated by the body in addition to baseline gravity effects

Measurement
	Acceleration		Raw acceleration as measured by the accelerometer
	Angular Velocity	Angular velocity derived from raw acceleration
	Linear Acceleration	Linear acceleration derived from raw acceleration

Dimensionality
	Magnitude	Pure magnitude regardless of X-Y-Z dimension
	X, Y, Z		The dimension measured

Statistical transformation
	mean		The arithmetic mean of the value
	std		The standard deviation of the value
